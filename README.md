# Kenvue (kenvue)
Kenvue is the world's largest pure-play consumer health company by revenue, formed from the consumer health spin-off of Johnson & Johnson. Everyday care is a powerful catalyst in making you feel better, inside and out. Kenvue's iconic brands include Tylenol, Neutrogena, Listerine, Aveeno, Band-Aid, and Johnson's.
